WHAT EXACTLY OUR JOURNAL OFFERS?

Storing Photos ...

You can store photos from all of yours small and big trips without fear of losing any data. We use Cloudinary to provide you with a quick photos uploading and downloading service.

... Places ...

We remember exact places where Your photos where taken so you can go back to those wonderful places and see where you have been!

... and more!

As well as storing photos and places we have bunch of other stuff like journey date, social sharing or gallery with favourite photos from all your journeys!

HOW DOES IT WORKS?

Create Journey

Get Our FREE Android App and create an account. You can sign in using your Facebook as well. Then create a new Journey, give it a title, description, start and end date.

Take photos

Take photos using Our App while having your trip. You can also use your phone camera and upload images from gallery. You can as well create trips from the past and upload old photos.

Manage, edit, share!

You can view, edit and share all of your images from almost any device. Forget about fulled micro-SD or lost data. We take care of your photos.
